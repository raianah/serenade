About This Project

Inspired by Receiptify, Serenade is a multipurpose tool that allows users to create 'virtual date cards' for their loved individual and their dates, and displays a user's top most played tracks on Spotify & Last.fm (soon).

Serenade Music Recap was added 3 days prior to release of the web application. It's initial purpose is only to create date cards. The creator believed that adding your music taste might enhance your date chance.

Originally created just for a certain individual under the name 'WYDM (Will You Date Me)', was made public to impress your date.
